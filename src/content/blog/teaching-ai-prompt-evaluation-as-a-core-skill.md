Prompting is often presented as a trick for getting better results quickly. In education, a better framing is to treat prompting as part of a broader evaluation workflow.

## Core classroom shift

Learners should inspect the relationship between task definition, prompt quality, output quality, and verification effort.

## Suggested activity

Have students write two prompts for the same task, compare the output quality, and explain which prompt creates the stronger result and why.

## Why this matters

This turns AI usage into a discipline of judgment, not just experimentation.
