This checklist turns AI usage into a more reflective process by encouraging students to inspect accuracy, relevance, and evidence before trusting an output.

## What students can learn

- How to check claims and detect weak reasoning.
- How to compare outputs against the original task.
- How to distinguish fluent text from dependable content.

## Recommended classroom use

1. Ask students to generate an AI answer for a course-related task.
2. Run the checklist together or in groups.
3. Revise the answer and discuss what changed.
