This module helps turn AI from a vague trend into a structured learning conversation for students and professional learners.

## Included focus areas

- prompt engineering basics for non-technical learners
- risk framing: bias, hallucination, and misuse
- integrating AI outputs into human-reviewed academic workflows

## Teaching note

Encourage students to evaluate both the usefulness and the limitations of generated output in a documented exercise.
