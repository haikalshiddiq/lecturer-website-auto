This lab starter helps students understand how communication protocols translate abstract models into observable traffic behaviour.

## Lab outcomes

- Identify the role of each layer in a communication exchange.
- Observe protocol handshakes and packet framing patterns.
- Build a habit of troubleshooting with evidence instead of guesswork.

## Suggested activity

Pair the download with a packet capture exercise and require students to annotate one real request-response sequence.
