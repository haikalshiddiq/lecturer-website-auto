This resource is designed to support classroom discussion around governance structures, digital operating models, and measurable performance.

## What students can learn

- How governance clarifies decision rights in digital transformation programmes.
- How service owners, process owners, and executive sponsors align.
- How KPI dashboards can be kept practical instead of becoming reporting theatre.

## Recommended classroom use

1. Introduce the governance canvas during a lecture.
2. Split students into groups and ask them to map a university service.
3. Use the KPI worksheet to compare strategic and operational indicators.
