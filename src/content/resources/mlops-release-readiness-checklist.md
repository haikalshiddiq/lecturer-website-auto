This checklist helps students understand that AI delivery quality depends on more than pushing code. It includes release evidence, fallback plans, and post-release observation.

## What students can learn

- How evaluation thresholds can become release gates.
- Why prompt or model regressions should be checked before deployment.
- How monitoring plans support safe AI adoption.

## Recommended classroom use

1. Use the checklist after an applied AI project sprint.
2. Ask groups to justify each release gate they include.
3. Compare operational risk between code-only and model-aware deployment plans.
