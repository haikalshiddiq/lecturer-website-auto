This lab turns release automation into a communication-protocol exercise that students can reason about step by step.

## What students can learn

- Which protocols are active during build, deploy, and post-deploy verification.
- How handshake, routing, and certificate failures affect release outcomes.
- Why observability signals matter during automated delivery.

## Recommended classroom use

1. Walk through a deployment path from repository to edge delivery.
2. Ask students to annotate each protocol interaction.
3. Use failure scenarios to discuss troubleshooting logic.
