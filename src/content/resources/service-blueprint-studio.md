This guide helps students visualise how information systems support institutional services from request to resolution.

## What students can learn

- How service steps, stakeholders, and decisions interact.
- How bottlenecks appear between process design and digital systems.
- How blueprint thinking supports governance and service improvement.

## Recommended classroom use

1. Select one campus service with clear user touchpoints.
2. Ask students to map actors, systems, and delays.
3. Use the blueprint to discuss improvement priorities.
