This resource gives students structured cases for interpreting how transport and session expectations affect user experience and system behaviour.

## What students can learn

- When reliability matters more than speed.
- How sessions shape continuity and coordination.
- Why protocol choice influences application behaviour.

## Recommended classroom use

1. Assign different scenarios to small groups.
2. Ask each group to justify the protocol logic they would prioritise.
3. Compare trade-offs across use cases.
